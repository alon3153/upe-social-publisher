### Section 6 — FAQ

**What is new hire team building?**
New hire team building is a structured programme — typically 1–3 days — designed to integrate recently joined employees into a team through shared experiences, facilitated activities, and social moments. The goal is faster cultural alignment, stronger peer relationships, and improved retention.

**How is this different from a regular team-building event?**
Standard team building often targets existing teams. New hire programmes are specifically designed for the onboarding phase — experiences, language, and content are calibrated for people who don't yet know each other and need to build trust quickly.

**Can the programme include formal onboarding content (presentations, training)?**
Yes. Many clients combine a half-day structured company presentation with 1.5 days of experiential team building. We coordinate with your HR or L&D team to integrate formal content naturally.

**How many people does UPE handle for new hire team building?**
Our range is 15–500. For large global cohort programmes (100+), we offer split-group parallel activity formats that maintain an intimate feel at scale.

**What does a new hire team building programme in Spain cost?**
Programmes range from €350–€1,200 per person depending on duration, format, accommodation, and production level. We provide transparent indicative budgets within 48 hours of a brief.

**What is an international product launch event?**
An international product launch event is a live, produced experience designed to introduce a new product, service, or brand to a target audience across multiple countries or markets. Spain — particularly Barcelona and Madrid — offers exceptional international air connectivity, exclusive landmark venues, a professional event infrastructure, and a media environment that gives European and global launches high visibility. **How much does an international product launch event cost?**

**Can UPE handle hybrid or livestreamed product launches?**

For flagship international productions: 16–24 weeks. For press-only or 50-person launches: 8–12 weeks. Exclusive venue hold can typically be secured in 2–3 weeks from briefing.

**Does UPE work with the brand's marketing agency?**
