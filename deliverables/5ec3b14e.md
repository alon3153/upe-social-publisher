### 🏢 FROM THE FIELD: WHAT WE SAW LAST QUARTER

In the last 90 days, we produced events across four continents. Here's what the data showed us:

**On Incentive Travel:**
The moment that always surprises clients is when we calculate what it actually costs to *not* run an incentive program. Replacing a senior employee costs between 50% and 200% of their annual salary. When your top 5% performers consider leaving — and Gallup tells us 50% of employees are currently watching for new opportunities — the math shifts dramatically.

A well-produced incentive trip to a destination that resonates isn't a luxury line item. It's retention insurance.

**On Corporate Conferences:**
78% of organizers now rate in-person conferences as their most impactful marketing channel in 2026 — ahead of paid media and digital advertising. Yet in our pre-production meetings, the question we hear most is: *"How do we prove this to the board?"*

The answer isn't poetry. It's a measurement framework built *before* the event begins, not after.

The three metrics we recommend every client track from day one:
1. **Pipeline impact** — deals accelerated or closed within 90 days of the event
2. **Retention delta** — engagement scores among event participants vs. non-participants 6 months later
3. **Net Promoter Score** — measured at event close AND at the 60-day mark

---

### 📌 BENCHMARK TO STEAL THIS MONTH

**The "Cost per Converted Relationship" metric**

Most companies calculate event ROI as: revenue directly attributed ÷ total event cost.
That's correct — but incomplete.

The metric that CMOs we work with have started using: **Cost per Converted Relationship (CCR)** — total investment ÷ number of attendee relationships that moved from cold/warm to active pipeline within 90 days.

When you calculate it this way, a €200,000 conference that converted 40 relationships = **€5,000 per converted relationship** — which, in B2B contexts, is frequently cheaper than a cold outbound pipeline at scale.

Put this metric in your next budget presentation. You'll get a different conversation.

**על טיולי תמריץ:**

טיול תמריץ מוצלח ליעד שמדבר לעובדים אינו סעיף תקציב יוקרתי. הוא ביטוח שימור.
