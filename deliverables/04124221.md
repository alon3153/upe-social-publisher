**Q: Can UPE handle both the creative concept and the full-day logistics?**

A: This format extends reach while preserving impact, particularly for distributed teams or global stakeholder groups. ### The Business Case for a Dedicated New Hire Team Building Event

HR and People leaders often underestimate what a well-produced onboarding event does commercially:

**Retention ROI.** Effective onboarding activities help new team members feel welcomed, supported, and equipped to succeed in their new jobs. A structured onboarding process can significantly improve employee engagement, productivity, and retention. Replacing a single mid-level hire costs 50–200% of their annual salary. One strong team building event pays for itself in retention.

**Speed to productivity.** With a thorough understanding of their role, company culture, and available resources, new hires can become productive more quickly. Proper onboarding reduces the learning curve and enables new employees to contribute effectively to their team's goals sooner.

**Culture transmission.** New employees learn about the company's values, norms, and behavioural expectations, helping them assimilate into the company culture faster. They start building a sense of belonging and successfully navigate social and professional interactions within the company.

**Employer brand signal.** How you welcome someone is how they describe your company to everyone they know. A memorable onboarding experience is content — for LinkedIn, for referrals, for talent pipelines.
