**Slide 1 — Hook:**
> **"Hybrid Event" ≠ "Event with a Zoom Link"**
> The 3 things that still break hybrid in 2026

**Slide 2 — Promise:**
> We've produced 1,500+ events.
> We've seen hybrid done brilliantly — and catastrophically.
> Here are the 3 patterns that consistently kill the remote experience.

**Slide 3 — Tip #1:**
> **#1: One production team, two audiences**
> Most events have one director focused on the room.
> The camera is an afterthought.
> Rule: assign a dedicated "remote producer" — someone whose only job is the stream.

**Slide 4 — Tip #2:**
> **#2: The "camera as wall" problem**
> A static wide shot of a stage = a terrible remote experience.
> Remote audiences need close-ups, graphics, and reaction shots.
> Think broadcast, not surveillance footage.

**Slide 5 — Tip #3:**
> **#3: Engagement asymmetry**
> In-room audience: networking, coffee, hallway chats.
> Remote audience: muted on Zoom with no one to talk to.
> Build 2–3 dedicated "remote moments" into the agenda. Not optional.

**Slide 6 — Proof:**
> UPE has produced hybrid events across 130+ destinations
> with 25,000+ participants since 2010.
> When hybrid works, it multiplies your event's impact.
> When it doesn't — it halves it.

## 7. כללי עיצוב לכל Carousel

כדי למקסם את dwell time: השקף הראשון חייב כותרת "stopping power" שפוגעת בנקודת כאב ספציפית, והשקף השני חייב מיד להבטיח פתרון או רשימת תובנות.

- **פורמט:** 4:5 portrait (mobile-first) — Portrait 4:5 הוא הבחירה החזקה ביותר לפידים מוכווני-מובייל.
- **לוגו UPE:** שקף 1 (קטן, פינה) + שקף 7 (גדול יותר)
- **פונט:** Bold + מינימום 28pt
- **שקפים:** 7 בדיוק
- **עקרון עיצוב:** עיצוב נקי עם רעיון אחד לשקף ויררכיה ויזואלית חזקה.
