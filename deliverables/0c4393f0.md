**Q: What is the best time of year for incentive travel in Spain?**
A: Spain is a year-round destination. Spring (April–June) and autumn (September–November) offer ideal temperatures for outdoor activities and lower peak pricing. Summer is excellent for coastal programs in Mallorca or the Costa del Sol. Winter is perfect for Madrid and Seville cultural programs.

**Q: How far in advance should we book an incentive trip to Spain?**
A: For groups of 50+, we recommend a minimum of 4–6 months lead time to secure preferred venues and hotel blocks. For 200+ participants, 9–12 months is ideal. That said, UPE has produced programs in under 8 weeks for clients with urgent needs — ask us about fast-track production.

**Q: What is "new hire team building" and why do companies do it in Spain?**
A: New hire team building is a structured corporate program designed to onboard and bond new employees quickly — accelerating their integration into company culture, building peer relationships, and aligning them to leadership. Companies choose Spain because its warmth, food culture, and diverse activity landscape create the ideal conditions for authentic human connection outside the office.

**Q: Does UPE handle flights and hotel as well as the event production?**
A: UPE is primarily a B2B event production company. We coordinate full logistics — including hotel block bookings and ground transfers — and can assist with airline block negotiations. We partner with corporate travel management companies (TMCs) for integrated flight booking when required.

**Q: Can UPE run hybrid events in Spain — combining in-person and remote participants?**
A: Yes. UPE has full AV and broadcast production capability for hybrid formats. Remote participants can join keynotes, workshops, and team challenges with dedicated tech production.

**Q: What makes UPE different from a local Spanish DMC?**
A: UPE combines the creative and production firepower of a global event agency with deep local execution expertise. We're not a tour operator or DMC — we're a full B2B event production house that builds bespoke programs from scratch, manages every creative and logistical element, and delivers measurable ROI reporting. 1,500+ events since 2010.

**Q: Is there a minimum group size for UPE Spain programs?**
A: Our sweet spot is groups of 30–600 participants. For smaller executive retreats (10–30 people), contact us — we can advise on the right format.
