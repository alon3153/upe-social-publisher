## מסגרת אסטרטגית (להתאמה לפני אישור)

> **רציונל:**
> Personal-profile organic reach runs roughly 5–10x Company Page organic reach for similar content. The 2026 algorithm update prioritizes authentic engagement over reach, video content over static posts, and personal connections over corporate broadcasting. Inbound outreach from personal content converts at 14.6%, while traditional cold outbound converts at 1.7% — פער שהופך personal branding מ"nice to have" לנשק פייפליין עיקרי.

> **טקטיקת פרסום:** Post 3–4 times per week consistently. LinkedIn's algorithm gives each post a 24–48 hour window, so posting more than twice per day cannibalizes reach. The founders who generate the most inbound publish on Tuesday, Thursday, and Friday.

> **עיתוי אופטימלי:** Tuesday through Thursday between 15:00–16:00 UTC are the highest-engagement windows for B2B content in 2026 — לקהל ישראלי ואירופאי: 17:00–18:00 IL / 16:00–17:00 CET.

> **קריטי — לגבי לינקים:** Posts with external links now receive 40% less initial reach. Post native content first, add links in comments after engagement begins.

## ⚡ כללי ברזל לשני המנהלים

1. **אל תשים לינק בגוף הפוסט** — posts with external links receive 40% less initial reach; add links in comments after engagement begins.
