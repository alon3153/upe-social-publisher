## TECHNICAL SPECS (2026 ALGORITHM)

LinkedIn posts truncate at roughly 210 characters, so your hook must land before the "See more" cutoff. For text posts, 1,300 to 2,000 characters tends to perform best in 2026.

Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday consistently see the highest engagement rates.

Best posting times are Tuesday to Wednesday, 8–10 AM local time, with secondary peaks at lunch (12–2 PM).

If you must share a link, put it in the first comment — not in the post body.

Comments are weighted 15x more than likes by the algorithm, with meaningful comments (15+ words) carrying significantly more weight than short reactions.

Carousel posts (uploaded as PDFs) and short-form video consistently outperform other formats in 2026. Carousels generate 2–3x more engagement than single-image posts because each swipe signals interest to the algorithm and increases dwell time.

## PRODUCTION SYSTEM — 90 דקות בשבוע

Commit to a consistent posting schedule (3–5x weekly minimum).

**מודל "batch שבועי" — כל ראשון בבוקר:**

```
ראשון 09:00–10:30 (90 דקות):
├── 30 דקות: כתיבת 3 פוסטים לשבוע הבא (מבנה: hook → body → CTA)
├── 20 דקות: עיצוב carousel ב-Canva (תבנית קבועה!)
├── 20 דקות: צילום short video (טלפון, 60–90 שניות, backstage)
└── 20 דקות: scheduling ב-LinkedIn Native Scheduler (ב׳, ד׳, ו׳)
```

**כלים מומלצים:**
- **Scheduling:** LinkedIn Native Scheduler (בחינם, ללא עונש אלגוריתמי)
- **Carousel design:** Canva (תבנית UPE קבועה — לא לשנות לוגו/צבעים בכל פעם)
- **Video captions:** CapCut או Descript (auto-captions)
- **Analytics:** LinkedIn Native Analytics (בדיקה שבועית, יום ראשון)
